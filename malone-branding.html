Logo Design | Branding

The Malone Center focuses on educational, cultural, and advocacy programs aimed to connect communities across the globe.

Project Profile

I had the opportunity to create logo options for the Malone Center, (previously known as the Clyde Malone Community Center) located in Lincoln, NE. Working with information from intensive research, options were produced to represent what the Malone Center stands for—educating the community, representing cultures, and having available advocacy programs, such as breastfeeding programs for families.

With all this taking into consideration, multiple logo options including mockups were presented to the client via the agency’s CEO. The chosen logo currently is the current staple of the Malone Center, and you can view it in action here.
